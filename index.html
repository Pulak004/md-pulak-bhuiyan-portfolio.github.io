About Me

I'm a researcher and engineer based in Bangladesh, working at the crossroads of computational fluid dynamics, thermal systems, and autonomous robotics. My work spans numerical simulations in Ansys Fluent and COMSOL to real-world robotics development in ROS 2.

As General Secretary of RoboSUST, I contribute to building a culture of engineering innovation and collaborative research. I'm passionate about translating complex simulations into meaningful engineering insights.

Beyond the lab, I'm an IELTS aspirant, a cricket player with Team Turbochargers, and a bamboo flute learner — proof that curiosity doesn't stop at engineering.
